How Speed-to-Lead works

From inquiry to booked appointment — six steps, fully automated, under 60 seconds total.

01

Lead arrives

Inquiry comes in via website form, WhatsApp, Facebook, Google Ads, or landing page.

02

AI captures & enriches

Lead details are extracted, enriched with property/listing context, and scored for priority.

03

Instant response sent

Personalized SMS, email, or WhatsApp reply fires within seconds — with relevant property or service info.

04

Qualification begins

AI conducts a natural conversation to qualify budget, timeline, and requirements — no human needed.

05

CRM updated & routed

Lead is created in your CRM with full conversation history, enrichment data, and priority score.

06

Appointment booked

Qualified leads are offered a calendar slot. Booking is confirmed and synced to your calendar automatically.
